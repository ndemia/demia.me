About me

I had an itch that I was dying to scratch: I wanted to build things for the web, not just design them.

That's why after more than 5+ years as a UX / UI designer, and some more extra years as a graphic designer, I decided to pursue my long-time ambition of becomming a front-end developer.

I'm curious and passionate about learning. I've always managed to find my way through the internet. So that's why I'm following the self-learning path.

  • 🍕 I make my own pizza. I learned from my parents, who made it every Saturday night.
  • 🧘 I practice Vipassana meditation.
  • 📧 I subscribe to more newsletters than I should.
  • 🔪 I love cooking. I try to make one new recipe every week.
